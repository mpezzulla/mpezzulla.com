About

Hello! I am an Associate Professor at Aarhus University. My interests are rooted in fluid–structure interactions and shell mechanics. I am passionate about continuum mechanics and differential geometry, which I study via analytical, numerical, and experimental methods. Before moving to Denmark 🇩🇰, I lived, studied, and worked in Rome, Italy 🇮🇹; Boston, USA 🇺🇸; and Lausanne, Switzerland 🇨🇭. I am a member of the American Physical Society (APS).
