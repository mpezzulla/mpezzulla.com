    About me

    Hello! I am an Assistant Professor at Aarhus University. My interests are rooted in fluid-structure interactions and shell mechanics.
    I am passionate about continuum mechanics and differential geometry, which I try to study via analytical, numerical, and experimental methods.
    Before moving to Denmark, I lived, studied, and worked in Rome, Italy , Boston, USA , and Lausanne, Switzerland . I am a member of the American Physical Society (APS).
